Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide users with information about the best family-friendly locations for white-water rafting in Utah. It will provide information on where the locations are, when the best time of year is to go, and what is available at those locations. This will help those who visit our page to plan a white-water rafting activity in a specific area.

Audience

The target audience is going to be adults looking to plan a family-friendly white-water rafting experience. They will most-likely be someone between the ages of 18-50 who has kids or young family members who is looking for a fun activity. They will most-likely be accessing the webpage on a mobile device. Using a simple design that is easy to navigate and looks family-friendly will help these types of users to have a good experience on our site.
